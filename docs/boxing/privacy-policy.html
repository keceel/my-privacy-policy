Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

BoxingAR ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's front-facing camera to detect body poses (punching and dodging movements) using Apple's Vision framework. All camera processing is performed entirely on your device. No images, video, or pose data are ever transmitted to any server, stored on disk, or shared with any third party.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No images or video are recorded or stored
  • No pose detection data is saved beyond the current session
  • No account registration is required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are integrated
  • No advertising frameworks are used
  • No data is transmitted to external servers

3. Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game scores and statistics — stored in the app's local storage (UserDefaults)
  • Settings preferences — language, sound, vibration, and display preferences

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud backends. The App functions entirely offline.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data at all, no special provisions are necessary. However, we recommend that children use the App under parental supervision due to the physical nature of the gameplay.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is minimal risk of data breach. All locally stored preferences remain within the App's sandbox as managed by iOS.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can clear your game scores from within the App's scoreboard. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing page.
