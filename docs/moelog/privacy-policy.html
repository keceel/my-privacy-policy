隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年3月5日

概述

萌录（Moe Log）是一款纯本地运行的宠物相册应用。我们高度重视您的隐私，本应用不会收集、上传或分享您的任何个人数据。

数据存储

您在萌录中创建的所有内容，包括照片、相册、宠物信息和文字记录，均仅存储在您的设备本地。我们不运营任何服务器，也不会将您的数据传输到任何外部服务。

设备权限

萌录可能会请求以下设备权限，仅用于应用核心功能：

  • 相机 — 用于拍摄宠物照片
  • 相册 — 用于选择和保存照片
  • 麦克风 — 用于语音识别输入文字
  • 语音识别 — 用于将语音转换为文字

这些权限仅在您主动使用相关功能时调用，且所有数据处理均在设备本地完成。

第三方服务

萌录不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

数据删除

您可以随时在应用内删除任何照片、相册或宠物信息。卸载应用将永久删除所有本地数据。

儿童隐私

萌录不会有意收集任何用户（包括儿童）的个人信息。

隐私政策变更

如本隐私政策有所更新，我们将在此页面发布修订版本。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Moe Log is a fully offline pet photo album app. We take your privacy very seriously. This app does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data.

Data Storage

All content you create in Moe Log — including photos, albums, pet profiles, and text entries — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any servers, nor do we transmit your data to any external services.

Device Permissions

Moe Log may request the following device permissions, solely for core app functionality:

  • Camera — To capture pet photos
  • Photo Library — To select and save photos
  • Microphone — For voice-to-text input
  • Speech Recognition — To convert speech to text

These permissions are only used when you actively engage the relevant features, and all data processing occurs locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Moe Log does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete any photos, albums, or pet profiles at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all local data.

Children's Privacy

Moe Log does not knowingly collect personal information from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, we will post the revised version on this page.
